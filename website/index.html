What is Q.W.E.S?

Q.W.E.S., or the Quarantine Wellness and Entertainment System, is an all-in-one platform that takes care of your basic needs plus more. At its basis, Q.W.E.S. is the application that will allow for better connectivity as well as enhanced experience even at home. The all-in-one app raises awareness by allowing users to post announcements, stay updated with news, and look at stock predictions, ensures good health by suggesting better yoga forms through the analysis of submitted yoga positions as well as listing dietary recommendations, and increases efficiency by quickening the order of groceries and food from food trucks and restaurants. All in all, Q.W.E.S. is THE app to use during quarantine.
